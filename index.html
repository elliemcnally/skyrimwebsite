Game Play


Skyrim is an open world role-playing game developed by Bethesda Studios in 2011.

The story focuses on the player characters journey to defeat Alduin the World-Eater and help the inhabitants of the game world of Tamriel.The soundtrack is not only immersive, but nostagic for those having played the game. Every composition reminds the player of a notable moment or atmosphere, bringing fantasy to life.

The story of the game is also completly optional to complete. The player may choose to complete the main questline or simply wander the world as they please, the game ends when the player is done with the experience. As Skryim is an open-world roleplaying game, the player can choose their own adventure, sideing with whomever they choose.
